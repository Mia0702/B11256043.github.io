Canberra

Overview of Canberra

  • Established: 1913
  • Population: Approximately 400,000
  • Features: As the political center of Australia, Canberra is home to many government institutions, embassies, and national cultural institutions.

Main Attractions and Activities

  1. Parliament House
  2. Australian War Memorial
  3. National Gallery of Australia
  4. National Museum of Australia
  5. National Library of Australia

Sydney

Overview of Canberra

  • Population: Approximately 5,200,000

Main Attractions and Activities

  1. Sydney Opera House
  2. Sydney Harbour Bridge
  3. Bondi Beach

Brisbane

Overview of Canberra

  • Established: 1824

Main Attractions and Activities

  1. South Bank Parklands
  2. Lone Pine Koala Sanctuary

Adelaide
